INFO:

This is a place where you can enjoy with friends and family. Filled with over 70 patio cafes, dining venues, italian spots/ returants, and so much more.

A neighborhood full of vibrant culinary scenes, lively atmosphere, full of boutiques amici park, art galleries and italian heritage stylish vibe.
